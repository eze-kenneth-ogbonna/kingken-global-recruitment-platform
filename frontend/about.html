Our Story

Kingken Global Travel Agency Ltd

Kingken Global Travel Agency Ltd is an international recruitment company specialising in connecting verified African workers with employers across the Gulf region, including Kuwait, UAE, and Qatar.

Founded with a mission to create transparent, ethical, and scalable recruitment pathways, we bridge the gap between skilled workers seeking international opportunities and employers who need a reliable, fast-deployment workforce solution.

Our technology-driven platform ensures every candidate is properly screened, documented, and ready for deployment — while every employer receives only verified profiles that match their exact requirements.
