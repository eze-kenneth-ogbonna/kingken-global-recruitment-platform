## Overview

This guide documents the complete sales and marketing funnel for acquiring both workers (supply side) and employers (demand side) on the Kingken Global platform.

---

## 1. The 5-Stage Funnel

```
Stage 1: AWARENESS          Prospects learn Kingken Global exists
          │
          ▼
Stage 2: INTEREST           Prospects explore services, visit website, ask questions
          │
          ▼
Stage 3: LEAD CAPTURE       Prospect submits form, sends WhatsApp, or signs up
          │
          ▼
Stage 4: CONVERSION         Lead becomes paying employer or deployed worker
          │
          ▼
Stage 5: RETENTION          Client returns, refers others, gives testimonials
```

### Funnel Metrics to Track

| Stage | Metric | Target (Monthly) |
|-------|--------|-----------------|
| Awareness | Reach (Facebook/Google ads) | 50,000+ impressions |
| Interest | Website visits | 2,000+ visits |
| Lead Capture | Form submissions | 200+ candidates, 20+ employers |
| Conversion | Placements completed | 5–10 workers deployed |
| Retention | Repeat employer orders | 50%+ of active employers re-order |

## 2. Facebook Ads Strategy

### Target Audience: Employers

**Campaign Goal:** Generate employer leads (companies wanting to hire)

**Audience Settings:**
- **Location:** Kuwait, UAE (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah), Qatar
- **Age:** 25–55
- **Interests:** Business management, Human resources, Staffing & Recruitment, Entrepreneurship, Small business
- **Job titles:** Business Owner, HR Manager, Operations Manager, General Manager, Company Owner
- **Languages:** English, Arabic
- **Behaviors:** Engaged shoppers (indicates business activity), Facebook Page Admins

### Target Audience: Workers

**Campaign Goal:** Generate worker applications

**Audience Settings:**
- **Location:** Nigeria (Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt), Ghana (Accra, Kumasi), Kenya (Nairobi, Mombasa), Ethiopia (Addis Ababa)
- **Age:** 22–45
- **Interests:** Working abroad, International opportunities, Travel, Hospitality jobs, Domestic work, Gulf countries
- **Languages:** English
- **Behaviors:** Recently moved, Expat community members

### 3 Ad Types and Formats

#### Ad Type 1: Awareness Video Ad (Workers)

**Objective:** Brand Awareness / Video Views
**Budget:** $10–20/day
**Format:** 15–30 second video
**Script:**
```
SCENE 1: Show Lagos or Accra market scene
VOICE: "Tired of struggling to find good pay at home?"

SCENE 2: Show professional worker in Gulf setting
VOICE: "Kingken Global connects you with jobs in Kuwait, UAE, and Qatar."

SCENE 3: Show happy worker family
VOICE: "Free to apply. Thousands placed. Your future starts today."

SCENE 4: Branding screen
VOICE: "Apply now at kingkenglobal.com.ng or WhatsApp +96569048174"
TEXT: "Apply Free Today — www.kingkenglobal.com.ng"
```

#### Ad Type 2: Lead Form Ad (Workers)

**Objective:** Lead Generation
**Budget:** $15–30/day
**Format:** Image with lead form
**Headline:** "Get a Job in Kuwait or UAE — Apply Free Today"
**Body:** "Kingken Global is placing Nigerian, Ghanaian, and Kenyan workers in Kuwait, UAE & Qatar. Domestic workers, drivers, security, nannies, and more. Free application. No fees."
**CTA:** "Apply Now"
**Instant Form Fields:** Name, Phone (WhatsApp), Country, Position Interested In

#### Ad Type 3: Lead Form Ad (Employers)

**Objective:** Lead Generation
**Budget:** $20–50/day
**Format:** Image + lead form
**Headline:** "Hire Reliable African Workers for Kuwait & UAE"
**Body:** "Looking for domestic staff, drivers, or security guards in Kuwait, UAE, or Qatar? Kingken Global provides vetted, trained African workers. Fast processing. Trusted service."
