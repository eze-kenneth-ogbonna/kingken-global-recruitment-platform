## 2. Role Definitions

### Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

**Reporting to:** Board / Founders
**Direct Reports:** COO, Head of Legal/Compliance

| Responsibility | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
| Strategic direction | Sets 6-month and annual goals for the company |
| Investor/partner relations | Manages relationships with investors, bank partners, embassies |
| Final approvals | Signs off on large deals (> $5,000), contracts, legal agreements |
| Brand and reputation | Ensures company is represented professionally at all times |
| Performance reviews | Quarterly performance reviews for senior staff |
| Government liaisons | Maintains relationships with Nigerian and Gulf government offices |

**Daily Time Commitment:** 2–3 focused hours on strategy and relationships

**KPIs:**
- Monthly revenue growth
- Strategic partnerships secured
- Brand reputation (testimonials, referrals)

---

### Chief Operations Officer (COO)

**Reporting to:** CEO
**Direct Reports:** Head of Recruitment, Head of Tech, Senior Ops Manager

| Responsibility | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
| Daily operations | Oversees all day-to-day platform activities |
| Team management | Manages and motivates all direct reports |
| Deal oversight | Reviews and approves all deals before invoicing |
| CRM ownership | Primary owner of the Google Sheets CRM; ensures data quality |
| Employer relations | First point of contact for all registered employers |
| Reporting | Prepares weekly summary for CEO |
| Conflict resolution | Handles team disputes and candidate/employer complaints |
| Budget management | Controls operational budget, monitors ad spend |

**Daily Routine:**
```
08:00 — Review daily reports from previous day
08:30 — Check Google Sheets Dashboard for KPIs
09:00 — Review new employer leads (Lead status in Employers tab)
09:30 — Contact new employer leads via WhatsApp within 24h SLA
10:30 — Team standup (WhatsApp or call)
11:00 — Review deals pipeline; follow up on outstanding payments
14:00 — Review weekly targets progress
17:00 — Review team daily reports
18:00 — Submit COO summary to CEO
```

**KPIs:**
- Deals closed per month
- Employer response time (< 24 hours)
- Team daily report submission rate
- Outstanding payment balance (minimize)

---

### Head of Recruitment

**Reporting to:** COO
**Direct Reports:** Recruiters (x3), Country Managers

| Responsibility | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
| Candidate pipeline | Oversees all candidates from New to Deployed |
| Screening oversight | Reviews AI-scored candidates; approves borderline cases |
| Employer matching | Matches approved candidates to open job requests |
| Quality control | Ensures only qualified candidates proceed to employers |
| Recruiter management | Daily supervision of 3 recruiters' workload and performance |
| Country Manager coordination | Weekly calls with Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya managers |
| Documentation | Ensures all candidates have required documents before processing |
| Reporting | Daily report to COO on pipeline progress |

**Daily Routine:**
```
08:00 — Review Master Data: all Status = "New" entries
08:30 — Assign new candidates to individual recruiters (update col N)
09:00 — Check Pipeline tab: any candidates stuck > 3 days in same stage?
10:00 — Review employer job requests; identify matches
11:00 — Team review with recruiters via WhatsApp
14:00 — Approve candidates for Processing stage
16:00 — Update Job Requests with matched candidates
17:30 — Submit daily report to COO
```

**KPIs:**
- Candidates screened per week (target: 50+)
- Approval rate (target: 30% of screened)
- Time from New to Approved (target: < 5 days)
- Deployment rate (target: 10+ per month)

---

### Head of Tech

**Reporting to:** COO
**Direct Reports:** None (may manage external contractors)

| Responsibility | Detail |
|----------------|--------|
| Platform management | Maintains all 7 Google Sheets tabs, formulas, and structure |
| Zapier maintenance | Monitors and troubleshoots all 6 Zaps daily |
| Google Workspace | Manages team email accounts and access permissions |
| WhatsApp bot | Maintains WATI flows, templates, and keyword automations |
| OpenAI integration | Monitors AI scoring; adjusts prompts as needed |
| Website | Maintains website pages, forms, embeds, and integrations |
| Data security | Manages access controls; ensures no unauthorized data access |
| Reporting tools | Builds and maintains Dashboard formulas and charts |
| Error monitoring | Reviews Zapier error logs daily; escalates critical failures |
| Documentation | Keeps all technical documentation up to date |

**Daily Routine:**
```
08:00 — Check Zapier Task History for errors (all 6 Zaps)
08:30 — Verify WATI message delivery for previous day
09:00 — Check Google Sheets for data anomalies (blank rows, wrong formats)
10:00 — Address any tech issues raised by team
14:00 — Monitor Facebook Pixel and GA4 for tracking issues
17:00 — Monthly audit: first Monday of each month — full system check
```

**KPIs:**
- Zap uptime (target: 99%)
- AI scoring success rate (target: 95%+)
- System downtime incidents (target: 0)
- Tech issues resolved within 24 hours

## 3. WhatsApp Group Structure

Three separate groups for different communication needs:

### Group 1: Executive Strategy

**Members:** CEO, COO, Head of Legal
**Purpose:** High-level decisions, investor updates, strategic plans
**Rules:**
- No operational updates in this group
- Messages responded to within 4 hours
- Weekly summary posted by COO every Monday

---

### Group 2: Operations Daily

**Members:** COO, Head of Recruitment, Head of Tech, Senior Ops Manager, HR/Admin
**Purpose:** Daily operations, daily reports, urgent issues, tool alerts
**Rules:**
- Daily reports submitted by 6 PM
- Urgent issues tagged with 🚨
- Tech issues tagged with 🔧
- Read receipts enabled

**Daily messages in this group:**
- Team daily reports (all members by 6 PM)
- COO morning standup summary (by 8:30 AM)
- System alerts from Zapier or WATI failures
- New employer registrations (auto-forwarded from Zapier)

---

### Group 3: Recruitment Team

**Members:** Head of Recruitment, all Recruiters, Country Managers
**Purpose:** Candidate coordination, job matching, document requests
**Rules:**
- No employer or financial data in this group
- Candidate IDs only (no full names in group chats)
- Positive and professional tone always

**Daily messages:**
- New candidate shortlists
- Urgent document collection requests
- Deployment confirmations
- Weekly team performance updates
