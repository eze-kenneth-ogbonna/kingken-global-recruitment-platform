**Frequency:** Every Monday (covering the previous week: Monday–Sunday)
**Owner:** COO prepares and presents; CEO reviews
**Distribution:** CEO, COO, Head of Recruitment, Head of Tech, Senior Ops Manager

## Weekly Review Meeting Agenda (Optional — 30 minutes)

If the team holds a weekly review call:

1. **(5 min)** — Headline numbers review (COO presents)
2. **(5 min)** — Pipeline and recruitment update (Head of Recruitment)
3. **(5 min)** — Revenue and employer update (COO)
4. **(5 min)** — Platform health (Head of Tech)
5. **(5 min)** — Issues and blockers (all)
6. **(5 min)** — Next week priorities and action items (COO)

**Meeting cadence:** Every Monday, 9:00–9:30 AM (WAT)
**Platform:** Google Meet or WhatsApp call
**Attendance:** CEO, COO, Head of Recruitment, Head of Tech (minimum)
